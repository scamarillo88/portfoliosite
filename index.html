Hi, my name is Sabrina Camarillo

I'm a UI designer from Bend, Oregon

I am a UI/UX designer with a background in food and hospitality, where I worked as a pastry chef and general manager. That experience sharpened my creativity, problem-solving, and ability to deliver high-quality results under pressure. I now specialize in UX research, responsive web design, and wireframing with tools like Figma. I am passionate about creating clean, user-centered designs and excited to grow in a role where I can contribute and learn.

My Work

Thryve

Thryve is a mobile-first financial wellness app focused on intuitive expense tracking and goal-based savings. I applied user-centered design principles, wireframing, and mobile UI/UX techniques to create a clean, approachable experience that encourages better money habits.

Scientifically Sweet

Scientifically Sweet is a responsive web app that empowers users to discover, customize, and save baking recipes through an intuitive and playful interface. I applied user research, low- and high-fidelity wireframing, preference testing, and responsive UI design using Figma to craft a user-centric baking experience.
