About the Designer

Hi, my Name is Sabrina Camarillo

I’m a UI designer with a passion for crafting intuitive, user-friendly digital experiences—and a professional background that’s anything but traditional. Before diving into design, I spent years as a pastry chef, where I honed my creativity, attention to detail, and love for building experiences people enjoy.

Now, I bring that same mindset to UI design. Through hands-on coursework, I’ve developed skills in Figma, wireframing, prototyping, and user-centered design principles. I thrive in collaborative environments, welcome feedback, and always aim to create designs that are as functional as they are visually compelling.

When I’m not designing, you’ll find me baking, going on hikes, floating the river, or simply recharging at home with my plants, a DIY project, or some arts and crafts. Whether I’m in the kitchen or behind a screen, I’m always drawn to creating things that bring joy, clarity, and a sense of purpose.
