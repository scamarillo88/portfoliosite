About the Designer

Hi, my name is Sabrina Camarillo

I am a UI designer based in Bend, Oregon, with a background in culinary arts and operations. After years of designing experiences through pastries and events, I discovered a passion for crafting beautiful, functional digital experiences—and I’ve been hooked ever since.

What makes me unique is my ability to blend creativity with precision. I spent years managing kitchens and baking under pressure, where timing, presentation, and collaboration mattered deeply. Now, I apply that same energy to designing user interfaces that feel clear, supportive, and intuitive.

I recently completed CareerFoundry’s UI Design program with a specialization in Front-End Development. My toolkit includes Figma, Balsamiq, HTML, CSS, and GitHub, and I started to love combining strong visual design with clean, accessible code. I'm especially interested in product design and working with mission-driven teams to solve real-world problems.

I'm currently open to full-time, freelance, and remote opportunities in UI, UX, and Product Design. When I’m not designing, you’ll find me hiking the trails, caring for my houseplants, floating the river, or experimenting in the kitchen.
